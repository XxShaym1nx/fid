There are plenty of places to find cats if you just look around!

In The Wild

The wild is a great place for picking up cats -for free, no less! Though the cost of dealing with rabies and other wild diseases may be more expensive.

At an Animal Shelter

Animal shelters are great for adopting cats. One shelter I recommend is the one where Vincent came from- Orange County Animal Services. They have great cats for relatively cheap, only $40 (and I hear on somedays it's free to adopt!) Click on the icon below to access the OCAS website.


At A Pet Store

Alternatively, you could just buy a cat at a pet store, which is like the animal shelter, but way more expensive. Just go to the animal shelter! You'll save a life! Pet stores don't kill their pets!

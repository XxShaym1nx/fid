Everyone loves cats! But does that necessarily mean they know how to take care of cats?

Feeding Your Cats

Feeding your cats is the next most important thing of all to do, second to giving them water. Set a specific time 3 times a day for your cat to eat breakfast, lunch, and dinner so they can live comfortably and predictably.

Any generic brand name pet food will work for most cats, but in case of old cats, it is recommended you give them softer food so they can chew better.

Also I can't stress this enough: DO NOT FEED CATS MILK! ALL CATS ARE LACTOSE INTOLERANT, IT WILL KILL THEM!

Interacting With Your Cats

Cats love to play! Make sure to buy lots of toys so the two of you can play together. But don't buy catnip, or they'll get addicted!

Bathroom Time With Your Cats

Do not bother to bathe your cats. Cats hate water, and they bathe themselves with their own tongue! But do remember to clean their litter box at least once a day.

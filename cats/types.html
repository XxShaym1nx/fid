Educate yourself on types of cats so you know which one you'd prefer to adopt!

Scottish Fold Cats

Cute cat species, right? Well, it's not so cute when you learn the truth- much like Manx cats, scottish folds get their ears from deliberate inbreeding and genetic mutations. DO NOT ADOPT A SCOTTISH FOLD, YOU WILL BE SUPPORTING THE INBREEDING INDUSTRY!

Sphynx Cats

Be warned- sphynx cats can cost upwards of thousands of dollars to adopt! Though, they are fit to adopt if you are allergic to cat fur- of which the sphynx has none.

Siamese Cats

Siamese cats are quiet, peaceful, and very friendly with people. The ultimate go-to choice for adopting a cat. If you can, take one immediately.

Tigers

Full stop. Don't even bother with these.
